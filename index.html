METRO MANILA


Metro Manila is the country’s economic, political and cultural capital. And it’s chaotic in the best way possible. From the colourful, roaring jeepneys to the dancing traffic enforcers, to the karaoke machines blasting 80s ballads in the streets, everywhere you go is teeming with life.

PALAWAN

The Philippines last ecological frontier with plant and wildlife biodiversity this stunning archipelago-province regularly tops the travel lists for worlds best beaches and islands.

CEBU & BOHOL

A gateway to spectacular islands, dazzling beaches and adventurous activities – with everything from diving, canyoneering, and skydiving on offer.

BORACAY

Boracay covers only 10km², including farming land and preserved forest. Its main White and Bulabog beaches are on opposite sides of the island’s central area, the narrowest point of which measures just 1km.
Once well-known as a party island, this is now a laidback getaway destination perfect for everyone from couples to families.

ILOILO & NEGROS OCCIDENTAL

Dubbed the ‘City of Love’ for its friendly, gentle and soft-spoken inhabitants, the 7 districts all have their own character thanks to colonial influences.
